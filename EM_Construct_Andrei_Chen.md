# 五篇文献中的 Earnings Management：构念、操作化与道德/心理论述汇编

本文汇总 **五篇**文献：（1）Andrei et al. (2024)；（2）Chen et al. (2015)；（3）Wang, Cao & Ye (2018)；（4）Scholtens & Kang (2013)；（5）Li, Kim, Wu & Yu (2021)。**第一部分**回顾各文如何界定 EM、理论角色与操作化；**第二部分**归纳文献对 EM 的**道德**与**心理**叙事（何为主流、何为缺失）。

# 第一部分：构念定位与操作化

## 一、Andrei, Benischke & Martin (2024)

**文献信息**

- 题名：*Behavioral agency and the efficacy of analysts as external monitors: Examining the moderating role of CEO personality*
- 期刊：*Strategic Management Journal*, 2024, 45, 113–143  
- DOI: [https://doi.org/10.1002/smj.3548](https://doi.org/10.1002/smj.3548)

### 1.1 理论框架中 EM 的角色

- **主理论**：行为代理模型（**Behavioral Agency Model, BAM**；Wiseman & Gomez-Mejia, 1998），与 **大五人格（FFM）** 结合。
- **核心问题**：更积极的分析师推荐如何与 CEO 的**收入增加型盈余管理**（income-increasing earnings management）相关，以及 CEO 人格如何调节该关系。
- EM 在文中被定位为：CEO 在感知到**对个人财富的风险承担（risk bearing）上升时，可动用的短期应对手段**之一，用于应对分析师预期、缓解“达不到预期”的威胁。

### 1.2 作者如何界定“Earnings Management 是什么”

文中明确定义（脚注）：

- **收入增加型盈余管理**：在财务报告中运用**判断**，使**当期报告盈余更高**。
- **相对概念**：**收入减少型**盈余管理则指在一期压低盈余、以便在后续期间抬高盈余（Cohen et al., 2008）。

与**代理成本**的挂钩：

- 对股东而言，收入增加型 EM **提高代理成本**：涉及可能**误导利益相关者**的财务报告决策，从而**加剧信息不对称**（Healy & Wahlen, 1999）。
- 股东因此更难察觉促使管理层进行 EM 的底层问题，并可能据此错误卖出股票（Martin et al., 2019）。

与 **BAM** 的挂钩：

- **Risk bearing** 被界定为对**代理人财富的主观威胁**（perceptual construct）。
- 更积极的分析师推荐会提高短期盈余压力；CEO 若更重负面信息，会感知更高 risk bearing，进而更多使用**收入增加型 EM** 作为**缓解威胁的短期方案**（如满足/贴近分析师预期，Abarbanell & Lehavy, 2003; Irani & Oesch, 2016）。
- 文中亦回顾：BAM 文献中 EM 常作为应对**业绩指引未达成**等威胁的杠杆；既有研究多关注**薪酬设计**与 EM 的联系（Martin et al., 2019; Zhang et al., 2008）。

### 1.3 在理论中“代表什么”


| 维度             | 含义                                                   |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **构念类型**       | 结果变量（因变量）：**收入增加型**应计/报告裁量行为；被解释的是其对分析师推荐（及人格调节）的响应。 |
| **行为动机（文中强调）** | 在**外部监控**（分析师）情境下，CEO **损失厌恶**下的**威胁缓解**行为。          |
| **经济含义**       | 对股东：**代理成本**来源之一（信息扭曲、信息不对称）。                        |
| **与“印象管理”等标签** | 本文**未**以印象管理为主线；强调的是 **BAM + 人格** 与**外部信息**的主观解读。    |


### 1.4 操作化

- 用 **discretionary accruals（DA）** 代表盈余管理，基于 **Dechow et al. (1995) 修正 Jones 模型**。
- 文中强调该变量为 **earnings management 的代理（proxy）**。

## 二、Chen, Luo, Tang & Tong (2015)

**文献信息**

- 题名：*Passing Probation: Earnings Management by Interim CEOs and Its Effect on Their Promotion Prospects*
- 期刊：*Academy of Management Journal*, 2015, 58(5), 1389–1418  
- DOI: [https://doi.org/10.5465/amj.2013.0351](https://doi.org/10.5465/amj.2013.0351)

### 2.1 理论框架中 EM 的角色

- **主理论**：**CEO 继任**研究 + **印象管理理论**（Goffman, 1959; Leary & Kowalski, 1990 等）。
- **Leary & Kowalski 两成分模型**：**印象动机（impression motivation）** × **印象建构（impression construction）**。
- EM 被明确表述为 interim CEO 在继任竞争情境下的一种**印象建构手段**，用以向**董事会、股东**等**关键受众**传递有利形象，并服务于**争取正式任命**的**职业激励**。

### 2.2 作者如何界定“Earnings Management 是什么”

- **一般定义**：管理层试图**影响所报告盈余**的行为，采用 **GAAP 允许范围内**的会计方法（或变更方法）（Daley & Vigeland, 1983; Dechow & Skinner, 2000）。
- **具体手段举例**：确认一次性/nonrecurring 项目、推迟或提前确认费用或收入等，以影响短期盈余。
- **与印象管理的联系**：此类尝试被既有研究视为有效的**印象管理策略**（Davidson et al., 2004）。

**文中刻意区分三类“盈余相关行为”**（脚注）：


| 类型                                        | 说明                                                 |
| ----------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Within-GAAP earnings management**（本文焦点） | 与**操纵、重述**在幅度、意图、经济重要性及是否违反 GAAP 上不同；**法律后果相对最小**。 |
| **Earnings manipulations**                | SEC 查处对象，**严重违反 GAAP** 的错报。                        |
| **Earnings restatements**                 | 常含不重要错报与无意错误，**法律/声誉**后果可能仍严重。                     |


另：**discretionary choices** 在 **within-GAAP** 框架下影响盈余（Davidson et al., 2004; DeFond & Park, 1997; Healy & Wahlen, 1999）。

### 2.3 在理论中“代表什么”


| 维度        | 含义                                |
| --------- | --------------------------------- |
| **构念类型**  | 解释**晋升概率**的**核心行为策略**之一（兼受治理等调节）。 |
| **理论标签**  | **印象管理战术**；**印象建构**的具体工具。         |
| **代表的行为** | 战略裁量受限、任期短时，用**账面业绩**快速塑造“改善”信号。  |
| **规范含义**  | 晋升若**过度依赖**被管理盈余，可能**损害股东长期利益**。  |


### 2.4 操作化

- **Discretionary accruals**（Dechow et al., 1995 修正 Jones；语境含 Kothari et al., 2005）。
- **正值** = income-increasing；稳健性含 **loss avoidance** 等。

## 三、Wang, Cao & Ye (2018)

**文献信息**

- 题名：*Mandatory Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Reporting and Financial Reporting Quality: Evidence from a Quasi-Natural Experiment*
- 期刊：*Journal of Business Ethics*, 2018, 152(1), 253–274  
- DOI: [https://doi.org/10.1007/s10551-016-3296-2](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10551-016-3296-2)

### 3.1 理论框架中 EM 的角色

- **准自然实验**：2008 年起沪深交易所对部分公司**强制**披露 CSR 报告；用 **DiD** 考察强制 CSR 对**盈余管理幅度**的影响。
- **核心结论取向**：强制 CSR 披露后，处理组 **|DA|** 下降；机制叙述为**信息不对称下降**、**监督与被发现概率上升**，从而**抑制**盈余管理。
- **自愿 CSR 的内生性讨论**：自愿披露与 EM 均为管理层自选择——CSR 可能**信号**高质量报告，也可能**掩饰（camouflage）**盈余管理（引 Hemingway & Maclagan 等）。

### 3.2 “Earnings Management 是什么”

- EM **掩盖真实业绩**、损害**资源配置效率**；用 **|discretionary accruals|** 表示**盈余管理幅度**（向上与向下均计入）。
- 补充分析涉及**向上盈余管理**与**监管处罚**的关联（Probit；**signed DA**、舞弊样本等设定见原文）。
- **替代指标**：**盈余平滑（earnings smoothing）** 等（Francis et al., 2004 类方法）。

### 3.3 操作化

- **|DA1|**：Kothari et al. (2005) **业绩匹配**修正 Jones；**|DA2|**：Raman & Shahrur (2008) **业绩与增长**调整；附录给出回归式。

## 四、Scholtens & Kang (2013)

**文献信息**

- 题名：*Corporate Social Responsibility and Earnings Management: Evidence from Asian Economies*
- 期刊：*Corporate Social Responsibility and Environmental Management*, 2013, 20(2), 95–112

**文件说明**：若本地存在文件名含 `1.pdf` 的副本，抽取结果可能**误对应其他文献**；正文归纳以 **18 页版** `Scholtens & Kang_2013_...Economies.pdf`（无 `1`）为准。

### 4.1 理论框架中 EM 的角色

- **主问题**：亚洲情境下 **CSR**、**投资者保护**与 **EM** 的关系；假设 **CSR 与 EM 负相关**、**投资者保护与 EM 负相关**（关系具**情境敏感性**）。
- **理论资源**：**机构利益相关者理论**、CSR 的多维责任（**environmental, ethical, social, governance**）、**代理成本**与**信任**文献。

### 4.2 “Earnings Management 是什么”

- 引用 Healy & Wahlen (1999) 等对 EM 的定义；强调 EM 为**代理问题**表现；作者明确将 EM 界定为**不道德且不负责任**，并与**恰当 CSR** **不一致**（原文表述：*earnings management as an act of irresponsibility and as inconsistent with appropriate CSR*）。
- **多指标**：除应计外，尚使用**盈余平滑、盈余激进**等**多种 EM 代理**以反映 EM 测度的**争议性**。

### 4.3 操作化

- 多种 **EM 指标**（平滑、激进等），回归设定参考 **Chih et al. (2008)** 等；具体变量定义见原文表与方法节。

## 五、Li, Kim, Wu & Yu (2021)

**文献信息**

- 题名：*Corporate Social Responsibility and Financial Fraud: The Moderating Effects of Governance and Religiosity*
- 期刊：*Journal of Business Ethics*, 2021, 170(3), 557–576  
- DOI: [https://doi.org/10.1007/s10551-019-04378-3](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10551-019-04378-3)

### 5.1 理论框架中 EM 的角色

- **主因变量**为**财务舞弊（financial fraud）**，非 DA 估计的 EM；但**引言与文献综述**大量讨论 **CSR 与 earnings management / manipulation** 的**两派观点**（伦理承诺 vs. 声誉掩饰）。
- **动机**：认为**模型估计的 EM** 测度误差大、难以可靠识别“操纵”，故以**已证实舞弊**企业为样本，观察舞弊期间 **CSR 表现**的动态变化。

### 5.2 “盈余操纵 / EM 在对话中是什么”

- **舞弊** = **更明确的**盈余操纵/错报证据；CSR 在舞弊期**显著升高**，解释为与舞弊**策略性协调**、**窗口粉饰（window-dressing）** 声誉以降低**举报与调查**风险。
- 与 **Kim et al. (2012)**（CSR 与盈余质量）、**Prior et al. (2008)**（CSR 与 EM）等**直接对话**。

### 5.3 操作化

- **CSR**：KLD 等 **CSR 绩效/强度**（非 DA）；**舞弊**：公开披露的欺诈事件样本；**治理、宗教环境**为调节变量。

# 第二部分：道德（morality）与心理（psychology）文献怎么说

## 1. Andrei et al. (2024)

**心理学（相对充分）**

- **BAM + 前景理论**：**损失厌恶**；**risk bearing** 为主观**感知**构念。
- **人格（FFM）**：调节对分析师信息的解读与是否动用 **income-increasing EM**；含**情绪稳定性**等与焦虑、压力相关的论述。
- **脚注**：EM 亦可能损害长期**声誉**；**短视损失厌恶**下 CEO 可能愿以长期风险换短期缓解——属**跨期权衡**，非道德哲学展开。

**道德（多为间接）**

- **无**系统“商业伦理”或规范伦理学框架；主要通过**误导**、**信息不对称**、**代理成本**（Healy & Wahlen）表达**对股东不利**的规范含义。

---

## 2. Chen et al. (2015)

**心理学**

- **印象管理**（动机 + 建构），**非**道德许可、道德推脱等**道德心理学**传统。
- 职业激励、受众对“业绩”的感知与 **leadership romance** 等**社会认知**线索有涉及，但**非**个体道德推理过程建模。

**道德**

- **Within-GAAP** 与 **操纵 / 重述** 的**合规谱系**区分；**opportunistic earnings management**；治理可抑制**机会主义**。
- **Graham et al. (2005)** 引述：管理层在 GAAP 内**积极**操作以降低外界对前景的不确定感——**规范张力**在“规则内策略”与**股东长期利益**之间。

---

## 3. Wang, Cao & Ye (2018)

**心理学**

- **几乎无个体心理**：机制为**信息不对称**、**关注与监督**、**被发现概率**、**威慑**。
- 间接引用 **Kim et al. (2012)**：**诚实类型**信号（CSR 与 EM 的效用结构）——仍为**信号/激励**，非道德许可。

**道德**

- EM **掩盖业绩**、**效率损失**；**integrity / trustworthiness**、**内部控制**与盈余可信；自愿 CSR **掩饰** EM 的假说；制度背景中的**商业道德**表述。

---

## 4. Scholtens & Kang (2013)

**心理学**

- **无**个体层面的认知/情绪/人格理论；使用 **agency、moral hazard（制度语境）**、**信任**等**经济—制度**语言。

**道德**

- **最直接**：EM 为**不道德且不负责任**；并明确 **EM 与恰当 CSR 不一致**（**不负责任行为**）。
- CSR 含 **ethical** 维度；EM 损害利益相关者对企业声明的**信任与信心**。

---

## 5. Li, Kim, Wu & Yu (2021)

**心理学**

- **非**实验心理学：强调**自利**、**策略协调**、**声誉与窗口粉饰**、**降低怀疑与举报风险**；**宗教环境**作为**利益相关者价值观**背景，解释 CSR 被**策略性滥用**的激励。

**道德**

- **文献层面**：CSR 与 **EM/操纵**关系——**伦理承诺**叙事 vs. **误导与掩饰**叙事。
- **贡献表述**：CSR **亦可被滥用**，具有 **ethical implications**；与“CSR 代表好文化”文献**对照**。

---

## 6. 五篇对照：道德 vs. 心理


| 文献                      | “道德”叙事            | “心理”叙事                |
| ----------------------- | ----------------- | --------------------- |
| Andrei et al. (2024)    | 误导、代理成本（间接）       | 损失厌恶、人格、威胁感知（**较充分**） |
| Chen et al. (2015)      | 合规分层、机会主义、股东福利    | 印象动机/建构（**非**道德心理）    |
| Wang et al. (2018)      | 诚信、掩饰假说、信息扭曲      | 信息环境与威慑（**弱个体心理**）    |
| Scholtens & Kang (2013) | **明文：不道德、不负责任**   | 几乎无（制度/代理/信任）         |
| Li et al. (2021)        | CSR—舞弊协同的**伦理含义** | 策略与声誉；宗教为**环境**而非个体心理 |


若研究强调 **moral licensing**：五篇中**均无**该机制；**Scholtens** 与 **Li** 更适合讨论 **CSR 与盈余不当行为的规范张力**；**Andrei** 适合 **BAM/威胁—DA**；**Chen** 适合 **印象管理与职业激励**；**Wang** 适合 **强制披露、信息不对称与抑制 EM**。

### Andrei, Benischke & Martin (2024, *SMJ*)

**摘要（人格与威胁）**

> We highlight the role of personality in shaping how CEOs respond to analyst recommendations, leading to boundary conditions for the efficacy of analysts as external monitors. We theorize that the extent to which a CEO perceives a threat from more positive analyst recommendations is contingent upon their personality, which shapes their subjective interpretation of the recommendation and their use of income-increasing earnings management in response.

**行为代理与损失厌恶（正文）**

> The BAM argues that CEOs are loss averse, meaning that they are more sensitive to personal losses than gains. The BAM framework proposes that as agent (CEO) risk bearing, defined as “perceived threat to agent wealth” (Wiseman & Gomez-Mejia, 1998, p. 136), increases, they take action to reduce that threat.

**心理特质（贡献段）**

> Our findings contribute to the literature by clarifying how CEOs' psychological traits can explain how they interpret and respond to feedback from investment analysts.

**脚注：收入增加型 EM 与“误导”（道德—经济后果）**

> For shareholders, income increasing earnings management increases agency costs as it involves financial reporting decisions that mislead stakeholders, thereby exacerbating information asymmetry (Healy & Wahlen, 1999).

**外部信息与心理属性**

> Given that CEOs' subjective interpretation of such stimuli is likely to be shaped by psychological attributes (Gamache & McNamara, 2019; Strauss et al., 2003), we argue that agency cost implications of monitoring by analysts are contingent on the CEO's personality, which influences how they interpret this ambiguous information and associated consequences for their personal wealth.

**声誉与短视（脚注讨论）**

> We acknowledge that earnings management can also create a threat to the CEOs non-financial wealth (reputation) in the longer-term if used to the extreme (Martin et al., 2016; Zhang et al., 2008). However, the notion that distant losses are more heavily discounted (myopic loss aversion; Thaler et al., 1997) suggests the CEO is likely to accept longer-term risk to reduce short-term risk.

### Chen, Luo, Tang & Tong (2015, *AMJ*)

**within-GAAP EM 与操纵、重述（脚注 1）**

> We focus on “within-GAAP earnings management,” which differs from earnings manipulations and earnings restatements in terms of magnitude, intentions, and the economic significance of the accounting choices, and whether such choices violate GAAP. The U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) identifies and punishes earnings manipulations. They represent the most egregious financial misstatements that materially violate GAAP. … while both earnings manipulations and earnings restatements can have serious legal implications for senior executives … within-GAAP earnings management is associated with minimum legal consequences and is much easier to get away with (DeFond, 2012). Indeed, some executives have admitted that “they would work aggressively within the confines of GAAP to reduce the perception of uncertainty about their firms’ prospects” (Graham, Harvey, & Rajgopal, 2005: 50).

**印象管理与机会主义（引言—治理）**

> We further investigate whether internal and external governance mechanisms can moderate the relationship between income-increasing earnings management and interim CEO promotion. … We find strong support for these effects … Corporate governance researchers might be persuaded to incorporate such additional factors into the analytical landscape. … Because earnings management is closely related to accounting activities, directors can better detect managers’ opportunistic behavior when they possess financial or accounting expertise, or when they can devote more time to the focal firm.

**印象管理两成分（理论）**

> According to the two-component model, impression management involves two discrete processes: “impression motivation,” or the extent to which individuals seek to control how others see them; and “impression construction,” or the specific impressions that individuals seek to build (Leary & Kowalski, 1990).

### Wang, Cao & Ye (2018, *J Bus Ethics*)

**自愿 CSR 与掩饰 EM（引言）**

> For instance, managers may use voluntary CSR reporting either to signal their high-quality financial reporting, leading to a negative relation between CSR and earnings management, or to camouflage their earnings-management activities, resulting in a seemingly positive association between CSR and earnings management (Hemingway and Maclagan 2004).

**诚信、不当行为与威慑（机制段）**

> Other information in the CSR reporting may relate to different aspects of corporate governance, such as investor relations, creditor relations, and employee relations, which may reveal information on the integrity and trustworthiness of the management that helps outside investors to gauge the effectiveness of internal control over financial reporting. When high-quality financial information is disclosed to the public and more investor and media attentions are drawn toward the firm, any misbehavior of the managers is more likely to be detected and punished. Therefore, the mandatory CSR disclosure tends to reduce information asymmetry and prevent managers from manipulating earnings.

### Scholtens & Kang (2013, *Corp. Soc. Responsib. Environ. Mgmt.*)

**伦理判断与代理（引言）**

> Earnings management is an agency cost because managers pursue their own interest to the detriment of stakeholders (Baiman, 1990; Mouck, 2004; Koch and Schmidt, 2010). However, it also is unethical and therefore irresponsible behavior. ### Li, Kim, Wu & Yu (2021, *J Bus Ethics*)

**CSR 与 EM：两种对立观点（引言）**

> Some scholars argue that CSR activities represent a managerial commitment to ethical behaviors, and find that socially responsible firms are less likely to carry out earnings manipulations (Kim et al. 2012). Other researchers contend that CSR strategies are used by self-interested managers to boost their public reputation and mislead stakeholders in order to disguise their manipulations of earnings, and have documented a positive relationship between CSR and earnings management (Prior et al. 2008; Tran and O’Sullivan 2018).

**舞弊、自利与形象（理论论证）**

> … we suggest that self-interested managers use CSR strategies to maintain good relationships with key stakeholders and to manage their company’s corporate image to reduce public suspicion, and thereby avoid the detection of their financial fraud.

**窗口粉饰（window-dressing）**

> Therefore, it may be the case that managers engage in CSR activities to window-dress their company’s reputation and image, and thereby reduce the likelihood of detection of the fraudulent activities.

**伦理含义（贡献）**

> … this study reveals that CSR can also be misused as a strategic tool by self-interested executives. Hence, our findings have important ethical implications regarding CSR performance, suggesting that these kinds of activities should be analyzed and interpreted using a more comprehensive framework that incorporates additional dimensions (such as the governance environment documented in this study) to understand their real purpose.

**宗教文献与“策略性利用”**

> Our findings suggest that this feature of religious people may be exploited by corporate managers when they misuse CSR activities to disguise their earnings manipulation.

## 对你自己研究的简要提示

- **构念上**：五篇覆盖 **BAM+人格 / 印象管理 / 准自然实验+|DA / 多指标 EM+规范判断 / 舞弊+CSR 动态**；与 **moral licensing + 应计 EM** 对话时，需**自行搭桥**理论语言。
- **道德上**：从 **“不负责任/不道德”（Scholtens）** 到 **“掩饰与协调舞弊”（Li）** 再到 **“强制披露抑制机会主义”（Wang）** ，可与“**先善后因**”叙事对照或对立。
- **心理上**：仅 **Andrei** 有较系统的**个体层面**（人格、损失厌恶）；若强调**组织层面道德许可**，需在讨论中明确与 **BAM、印象管理、信息不对称** 文献的分工。
